---

## Integration note

Paper 1 intentionally operates at a global architectural level.

It describes broad regulatory parameters for theoretical tractability.

Later work (especially Paper 4) introduces finer-grained signal-class variation.

This is refinement, not contradiction.

Safer interpretation:

> Paper 1 describes first-order global regulatory architecture.  
> Later papers describe domain-specific modulation within that architecture.

---

## REL interpretation

Paper 1 introduces Regulation Externalization Latency (REL) as a structural timing parameter.

Later theory suggests that effective latency may vary across signal classes.

Example:

    shame-triggered externalization: rapid
    professional frustration tolerance: prolonged

Thus:

    REL = foundational timing construct
    signal-class modulation = later refinement

No contradiction exists.

Only increased granularity.

---

## EV interpretation

External vectors in Paper 1:

- validation seeking
- dominance
- shame defense
- withdrawal

should be interpreted as recurrent regulatory output families, not necessarily exhaustive canonical categories.

This preserves flexibility without weakening the mechanism model.

# Paper 3 — Narcissistic Organization

## Current role

Clinical application paper.

Answers:

**What does SRF look like in narcissistic organization?**

---

## Integration note

Paper 3 predates Signal-Class Architecture.

Therefore its explanatory granularity is broader than what later theory permits.

The core logic remains valid:

narcissistic organization as defensive regulation architecture.

But later refinement allows greater precision.

Instead of:

    global rigidity

later framing suggests:

    selective compression across high-threat signal classes

Example candidate:

    status resolution: high
    strategic reasoning: high
    shame resolution: very low
    dependency resolution: very low
    rejection tolerance: very low
    separateness resolution: very low

This substantially strengthens the model.

---

## Empathy interpretation

Paper 3 may benefit from refinement.

Instead of:

    global empathy deficit

Later theory suggests:

    channel-dependent or state-dependent empathy collapse

Meaning:

Empathy failure may emerge specifically when self-threatening signal classes activate.

This aligns much better with Papers 4–5.

---

## Dependency interpretation

Dependency Double Bind remains one of the strongest concepts in the current theory program.

Later refinement allows cleaner interpretation:

dependency-related signals may be structurally compressed.

This increases explanatory precision.

---

## Action

Highest retrofit value.

Recommended conceptual update before broader release.
